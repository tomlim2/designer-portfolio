Overview

Technologic is a stop-motion graphic that shows the progression of graphic design tools from pencil, what is an earlier analog design tool, to desktop, what is a popular digital design tool today.
My teammate Cooper Kao and I were motivated by historical progressions of imagery, and selected the song "Technologic" by Daft Punk; the introduction lyrics of the song had immersive words matching with our concept. The words in this video are a representation of historical progression, and the video shows how people have been developing design methods from handwriting on paper to computer graphics on the desktop.
