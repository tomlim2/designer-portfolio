Overview

Smile Factory is an interactive poster to show looping that we keep on smiling to socialize with other people, but people do not smile that much. A forged smile means persona to socialize, and the factory means a media to 'help' to make perfect smile such as social education, uTube, and so on. The factory makes people look the same as other people, but they come back to normal because people can not keep smiling, and often forget how to do that. Hence, they keep going Smile Factory to communicate with other people.
