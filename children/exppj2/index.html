Collaborating Art and Augmented Reality

project aug.art

Through aug.art, ‘art’ and ‘collaboration’ becomes redefinded; the piece takes on multi-layered, multi- dimensional qualities.



Aug Art is augmenting “2D”static art into a“3D”augmented reality animation using the Aurasma app and collaborating with the artists to create an experience. It is an experience that combines Aurasma’s augmented reality technology with traditional art forms to create unique, interactive, and informational experiences.

Thanks for all artists who participated in aug.art

My roles in this project was technology, making motion, animation, and scripting for AR engine, Aurasma.
