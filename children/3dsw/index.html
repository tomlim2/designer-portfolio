36 Days of Type

3D Motion graphic

Overview

36 Days of Type is a personal experimental project that explores visual language using current computer-generated imagery (CGI) software, such as Cinema4D, Blender, and After Effects. The goal is to make thirty-six different letters and numbers in thirty-six days.
I believed that different mediums have their own unique feelings that only they can express. To understand attributes of CGI from 3D modeling to rendering, I gathered diverse skills from research and references on Youtube, Instagram, and Dribble, then experimented with rules inspired by the 36 Days of Type challenge.
