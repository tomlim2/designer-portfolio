CUBE
Summer 2018

Overview

CUBE is a Virtual Reality experience that influences a user’s emotions through the activation of harmonic and dissonant sounds. As a user fills in an abstract grid, their actions trigger tones that engender either pleasing or discordant reactions.
Concept & Design
Jeremy Mende & Tom Lim

Sound Engineering
Kirk Johnson & Shengkai Zhao

Technology
Unreal Engine with HTC Vive

© Jeremy Mende & Tom Lim, 2018.
