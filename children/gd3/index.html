Naming

Olivia

This was the name of a Nymph. Olivia can derive from the laurel tree, meaning "symbol of peace".

As I want chickens to live in peace at least during life, and rest in peace, I named my chicken pasture Olivia. The Latin form of the ancient Greek name Olvia.
